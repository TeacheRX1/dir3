Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our premium store

After completing your purchase, you'll receive a confirmation email with redemption instructions. In-game, use the command /redeem [code] to claim your items. Premium vehicles can be collected from the Premium Garage, and skins can be accessed through the clothing menu.

All premium purchases are permanent and tied to your account. Even in the event of server wipes or updates, your premium items will be restored. The only exception is for seasonal or limited-time items, which will be clearly marked as such in their description.

Premium items are designed to be cosmetic or convenience-based only. While premium vehicles may have unique looks and sounds, their performance statistics are balanced to match similar non-premium vehicles. We maintain a strict no-pay-to-win policy to ensure fair gameplay for all users.

We offer refunds within 48 hours of purchase if you have not yet redeemed or used the items in-game. To request a refund, please contact our support team through the Discord server with your purchase details. Once items have been redeemed in-game, refunds cannot be processed.

We add new premium content monthly, with major updates every season. Follow our Discord announcements for previews of upcoming items and special limited-time offers. Premium store subscribers receive early access to new content before general release.
