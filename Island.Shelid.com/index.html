About Us

SahraCity was created with one goal in mind: to provide the Algerian gaming community with an authentic roleplay experience that reflects our culture, language, and way of life.

Started in 2023, our server has grown to become one of the most popular Algerian roleplay servers, with a dedicated team of developers constantly working to improve and expand the experience.

We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming environment for both experienced roleplayers and newcomers. Our community is known for its helpfulness and friendliness, making SahraCity not just a game, but a place to connect.
